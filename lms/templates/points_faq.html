About points
Announcing appliedx reward points!
Corporate learning just got a big dose of fun: you can now earn rewards any time you take a course on appliedx!
Little activities on appliedx will earn you ‘enlightenment points’ – get points when you enroll in courses, watch videos, attempt quizzes, recommend courses to colleagues and complete courses. As you add points, cool badges will appear on your appliedx dashboard. When you get enough points and badges, you can trade them in for tangible rewards Here’s how to track your points:
  1. No need to sign up: your appliedx ‘enlightenment points’ will start building up automatically as you complete certain activities on appliedx. For example, you get 1 point when you enroll in a course, 5 points when you watch a video, 10 points when you refer the course to a friend and a whopping 50 points when you complete it.
  2. To view your points, go to the appliedx dashboard. You can access this page by clicking either on the appliedx logo at the top left of the appliedx page, or your username at the top right.
  3. On the dashboard, you will see your points divided by category, points needed to get to the next level and all the badges you have earned.
  4. Hover over the different elements of the points dashboard and the badges to learn more about them!
  5. Look out for opportunities to convert the points and badges into rewards!
That’s it! Get started today
Frequently asked questions
What are points?
Points are a quick way to indicate how much time on appliedx is spent by an individual. It’s a learning indicator, the more you learn better will be your points.
How can I get points?
Points will be added up for activities you perform on appliedx. At present, your points will be improved for doing the following activities on a verified course -
  • Enroll to a course - 1 point
  • Solve a problem - 2 points
  • Watch a video - 5 points
  • Refer a friend - 10 points
  • Complete a course - a whopping 50 points
What is a verified course and how do I recognize it?
Verified courses are the courses created by highly skilled subject matter experts on request. These are verified and validated by the team of experts with one or more
QA sessions before releasing. You can recognize them easily by verified label in the course listing page as shown below –
How can I refer a friend?
You can refer a course to friend with your referral link. To generate a referral link, go to progress page and click Invite friend button as shown below -
A referral link is generated in the text box next to the button. Share this link to your colleagues over mail or IM.
When will the referral points credited to my account?
Every time someone enrolls to the course with your referral link, points are attributed to your account.
Is there a limit on referral points can earn?
You can refer any number of times and keep getting points for every new enrollment happens with your referral link.
I have completed few courses last week; do I get points for those?
No, you cannot get points for previous completions.
Do I get points for completing question, even if it is wrong?
No, you can get points only for a right answer.
I watched a video but did not get points, why?
You should watch complete video to get points. Please wait until the video ends.
